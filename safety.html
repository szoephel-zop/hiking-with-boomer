Dog Safety on the Trail

Keeping your dog safe on the trail is just as important as enjoying the hike. Always bring water, be aware of trail conditions, and make sure your dog is comfortable and secure during your adventure.

Hydration Break

Always bring water and a collapsible bowl to keep your dog hydrated on the trail.

Off-Leash Awareness

If a trail allows dogs to be off leash, make sure your dog is well-trained and stays close to you. Going off trail can expose them to hazards like sharp vegetation such as stickers or sheet-grass, snakes, or wildlife like denning coyotes.
